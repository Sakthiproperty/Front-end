Sakthi Property, part of the prestigious Sakthi Group, has been a trusted name in Chennai's real estate sector for over 20 years. We are renowned for our commitment to quality, timely project delivery, and architecturally exquisite homes that harmonize with their surroundings.

With a transparent and customer-centric approach, we have proudly served over 1,00,000 happy customers, offering premium-quality residences tailored to diverse lifestyles. Established in 1984, the Sakthi Group has expanded into steel, logistics, and industrial parks under the leadership of Mr. K.A. Anand.

All our projects are RERA-approved, ensuring secure and reliable investments, while our brands like SSI TMT and Sri Durga TMT set industry benchmarks for quality and excellence.
