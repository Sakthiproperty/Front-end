Our Services

Exceptional service delivered with care and reliability.

Sakthi Property, a proud member of the esteemed Sakthi Group, has been a trusted leader in Chennai’s real estate landscape for over two decades. Renowned for our unwavering commitment to quality, timely delivery, and architectural brilliance, we create homes that blend seamlessly with their surroundings. With a transparent, customer-first approach, we have earned the trust of more than 1,00,000 satisfied clients, offering premium residences tailored to meet diverse lifestyles and aspirations.

Founded in 1984, the Sakthi Group has evolved into a dynamic conglomerate with a strong presence across steel, logistics, and industrial parks. Guided by the visionary leadership of Mr. K.A. Anand, we stand for innovation, sustainability, and reliability. Every Sakthi Property project is RERA-approved, ensuring secure and trustworthy investments. Our sister brands, SSI TMT and Sri Durga TMT, set industry benchmarks with advanced manufacturing practices and a steadfast dedication to excellence.

Our Methodology

A structured approach focused on efficiency, quality, and timely delivery from start to finish.


1

Planning & Design

We begin with a detailed consultation to understand the client’s vision, budget, and site conditions. Our design experts craft innovative, sustainable, and functional layouts that meet both aesthetic and practical goals.

  • Feasibility study and concept design
  • Architectural and structural planning
  • Regulatory approvals and documentation
2

Foundation & Structure

Our engineering team ensures every foundation is strong, stable, and built with precision. We use high-quality materials and modern techniques to guarantee long-term durability and safety.

  • Site preparation and excavation
  • Reinforced concrete foundation works
  • Structural framing and load testing
3

Construction & Installation

Our skilled workforce manages every phase of the construction process with attention to detail. We integrate modern technologies for faster execution and ensure all systems meet industry standards.

  • Electrical, plumbing, and HVAC systems
  • Interior and exterior finishing
  • Continuous quality inspections
4

Completion & Handover

We finalize each project with thorough inspections, documentation, and client walk-throughs. Our goal is to deliver excellence that exceeds expectations on time, within budget, and to full satisfaction.

  • Final quality testing and verification
  • As-built documentation and warranties
  • Project handover and client training
