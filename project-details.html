Sakthi’s Tranquilis Overview

Welcome to Sakthi’s Tranquilis, a truly independent style 2 & 3 BHK apartment complex of 4 Blocks. These 205 lavish units are nestled among Palm and Silver Oak trees, surrounded by landscaped gardens and villas. With 3-side open layouts, the apartments offer cross-ventilation, natural light, and privacy.

Apartment sizes range from 1386 sq. ft. to 1761 sq. ft., designed to provide spacious living and grand interiors. Residents will also enjoy a double-storied clubhouse with world-class amenities.
