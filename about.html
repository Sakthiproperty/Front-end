About Sakthi Property

Sakthi Property, part of the esteemed Sakthi Group, has been a trusted name in Chennai's real estate sector for over 20 years. We are celebrated for unwavering commitment to quality, on-time project delivery, and architecturally splendid homes that harmonize with their surroundings. With a customer-friendly approach and transparency, we have proudly served over 1,00,000 happy customers, offering premium-quality residences tailored to diverse lifestyles.

Established in 1984, the Sakthi Group has grown into a versatile conglomerate spanning steel, logistics, and industrial parks. Under the leadership of Mr. K.A. Anand, we prioritize innovation, sustainability, and reliability. All our projects are RERA-approved, ensuring secure and reliable investments. Our brands like SSI TMT and Sri Durga TMT set industry benchmarks with cutting-edge manufacturing and rich standards of excellence.
